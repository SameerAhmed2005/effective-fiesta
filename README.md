# Fitness and Health Application

This is a command-line Fitness application designed to help students manage their health. The application features include a BMI calculator, a Calorie tracker and calculator, an activity tracker, a unit converter, and an AI-style fitness assistant with a quiz to test users on their fitness knowledge.

### 1 BMI Calculator

1) Calculate BMI
2) Save your BMI and view history with dates

### 2 Calorie Tracker

1) Calculate daily caloric goal 
2) Track your daily calories and compare with your goal

### 3 Activity Tracker

1) Log daily steps
2) View weekly steps and calories burned during the  week

### 4 Unit Converter

1) Kg to lbs
2) lbs to Kg
3) Cm to Feet
4) Feet to Cm

### 5 Ai-Style Assistant and Quiz

1) The assistant gives fitness advice based on keyword matching
2) Questions based on fitness knowledge
